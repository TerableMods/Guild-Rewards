## Info:

- Claims all _available_ guild reward boxes using a chat command.

---

## Install:

- **S_GUILD_QUEST_LIST.2.def** and **C_GET_GUILD_QUEST_WEEKLY_REWARD.1.def** (included files) to:
> TeraToolbox\data\definitions

- C_REQUEST_GUILD_INFO
- S_GUILD_INFO


---
- The *definitions* above must have their opcodes mapped for your region, download them here:
  - NA patch **102.2** => [protocol.378447.map](https://github.com/TerableMods/OP-codez)

- The *.map* file in the link above, must be placed inside of:
> TeraToolbox\data\opcodes

---

## Commands:

- **gbox** or **rewards**
